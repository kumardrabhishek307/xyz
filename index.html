Precision Laser Treatments

Our advanced dental laser system enables virtually painless gum contouring, cavity removal, and teeth whitening. Laser energy precisely targets tissue with no vibration, minimal bleeding, and dramatically faster healing compared to conventional drills.

  • ✦ Gum disease treatment & sculpting
  • ✦ Cavity preparation without drills
  • ✦ Professional teeth whitening
  • ✦ Soft tissue procedures

Digital X-Ray & CBCT Imaging

Our digital OPG and CBCT scanners provide 3D imagery of your jaw, teeth, and surrounding structures — enabling highly accurate diagnosis and implant planning with up to 80% less radiation than conventional X-rays.

  • ✦ 3D CBCT cone beam scanning
  • ✦ Digital OPG panoramic X-rays
  • ✦ RVG intra-oral sensors
  • ✦ Real-time implant planning

4-Tier Sterilisation Protocol

We follow a rigorous four-tier sterilisation process certified against ADA and OSHA guidelines — the gold standard in infection control, giving every patient complete peace of mind.

  • ✦ Tier 1: Ultrasonic instrument cleaning
  • ✦ Tier 2: Self-sealing sterile pouches
  • ✦ Tier 3: Class-B autoclave sterilisation
  • ✦ Tier 4: Sterilisation monitoring certificate

Digital Smile Design

Using augmented reality smile simulation, you can preview the exact result of your cosmetic treatment before we begin — ensuring complete satisfaction and confidence in your transformation journey.

  • ✦ AR smile simulation & preview
  • ✦ Digital veneer planning
  • ✦ Before & after visualisation
  • ✦ Custom treatment mapping
